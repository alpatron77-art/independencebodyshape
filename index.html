Safety & Accessibility

We take safety, accessibility, and organization seriously so members can focus on training with confidence. The facility is designed to be easy to navigate, well-lit, and professionally maintained at all times.

  • ADA Compliance: Wheelchair-accessible equipment and restrooms
  • Security: Well-lit workout areas with camera monitoring
  • Locker Storage: Secure locker areas for personal belongings
  • Organization: Safety-focused, clean, and clearly arranged equipment layouts

Whether you’re new to training or a longtime member, the environment is built to support safe, focused workouts for everyone.
